Bavaria R55

Specifications of Bavaria R55

The motor yacht Bavaria R55 is produced by the brand Bavaria since 2017. Bavaria R55 is a 17.69 meters planing yacht with flybridge with 3 guest cabins and the draft of 1.27 meters which can reach up to 34 knots. The yacht with fiberglass / grp hull has CE certification class (B) and can go not further than 200 miles off the coastline.

Overview of Bavaria R55

Exterior and interior design

The Bavaria R55 model from the sports line of the German shipyard was presented in early 2019 at the exhibition in Düsseldorf. The smooth and elegant hull lines reflect the power of a sports bridge yacht, but emphasize its elegance. The saloon is decorated in light beige tones, with cabins in matte wood and grey shades added to this colour scheme. Due to the extensive glazing, the interiors do not look dark, there is a lot of natural light.

Amenities on board

There is plenty of space on the boat for outdoor recreation. The compact but spacious flybridge has a lounge, grill bar and cocktail area. There is a sunbathing area on the foredeck and a spacious terrace aft, which can be connected to the salon if the doors are opened. On the lower deck there are three cabins for guests with separate bathrooms and dressing rooms. Another crew cabin is aft.

Features

Right on the bathing platform there is another barbecue area, which is compactly hidden in a lockable cupboard and does not disturb the boat's appearance. The platform itself is hydraulically driven and can be lowered into the water, it is also suitable for storing a tender or water scooter.

Swimming area

The Bavaria R55 motor yacht can accommodate up to 12 people and is suitable for a boat trip with friends or a small family cruise by sea.
